# Track: Temple of Speed

Monza-shaped: chicanes + flat-out straights. Largest track. Currently 349 points after the chicane refactors, Curva Grande rework, Lesmo II tightening, and Curva del Serraglio kink.

## Completed
- Centerline generated by `scripts/gen-tracks.mjs`, scaled 2× for chicane resolution.
- Three real chicanes:
  - **Variante del Rettifilo (Prima Variante)** — three-arc Monza-style: ~90° right (R=48) → 5u straight → ~135° left (R=48) → 20u straight → smooth ~45° right (R=248.55). Sum of angle deltas = 0; chicane returns to y=500 so the post-T3 straight lines up with the start-finish straight. Connector + Curva Grande remain at original positions.
  - **Variante della Roggia** — three-arc 30°-60°-30° (L-R-L). Returns to original line, exits at original (-1000, -250).
  - **Variante Ascari** — three-arc 60°-120°-60° along the diagonal direction. Returns to original line, exits at (300, 250).
- Other corners as arcs: Curva Grande (80°, R=180, wider sweeping arc; tilted back-straight to Roggia entry), Lesmo 1, Lesmo 2 (R=70, slightly tighter), Curva del Serraglio (5° left kink at R=400 mid-back-straight), Parabolica.
- Schema v2, default grass runoff (outside 80, inside 30).
- Gravel patches: outside arc-1 (T1 entry, south side), outside arc-2 (T2 escape area, north side), plus outside-arc gravel for Curva Grande, Lesmo 1, Parabolica.
- `MenuScene.TRACKS` entry; selectable from menu. `startIndex = 26`.

## Architecture Decisions
- **Real Monza shape over straight-line preventability via sin-wave.** The original sin-wave chicane forced cars off-line at the apex but produced self-intersecting offset polygons (the X-pattern walls). Switched all three chicanes to arc-based geometry; this both renders cleanly and matches the real track's shape.
- **Chicanes return to the same lateral line** so connector + Curva Grande + the rest of the track keep their original coordinates. Avoids cascading geometry changes.
